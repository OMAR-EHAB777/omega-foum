WHO WE ARE

Egypt for Panel and Steel Industries was founded in 2011. the factory
was set up inside the industrial zone in 6th of October city. Due to
enormous development in Sandwich panel production technology we
have established two production lines with the capacity of 4000 m2 to
supply the market with high quality product that comply with the
universal quality standard.

The company has experience of more than 25 years as an extension and
evolution of Egyptian companies seek to lead in the Egyptian market and
global. We strive for success and progress. Which depends essentially on
modern technology and skilled labor.Which we seek to develop and train
through continuous training programs.
